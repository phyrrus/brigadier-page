Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Brigadier collects, and the ways in which it uses that personal information.

Personal Information Collection

At Brigaider.club, if you sign up to receive the newsletter, you provide your name, surname and email address, plus ticker and percentage of the portfolio you would like to monitor. That is the only information we have for you and all we need to operate brigadier.club. We do not collect any information on other visitors to the website. We use third third-party vendors, identified below, to help with delivery of the newsletter.

Legal Basis for Collection of Information

Brigadier asserts the following legal grounds for the collection of data: 1) Explicit Consent - When you sign up for the newsletter, you provide explicit consent for Brigadier to collect the above listed personal information; 2) Legitimate Interests - Brigadier may have additional legitimate interests, including account maintenance and administrative purposes, for the collection of the above listed personal information.

Using Personal Information

Brigadier may use your personal information to fulfill or meet the reason you provided the information. Specifically, delivering the newsletter and account-related email. We may also use your personal information to: provide you with support and to respond to your inquiries, including to investigate and address your concerns and monitor and improve our responses; administer website and content. respond to law enforcement requests and as required by applicable law, court order, or governmental regulations; and send you marketing communications. Brigadier does not share any of its user data with any third parties except the third-party vendors identified below. In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified in this privacy policy, Brigadier may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Data Processors

Brigadier uses the following data processors with respect to the personal information collected: Microsoft Azure to host the website, deliver the newsletter, and all related user login data and analytics. Microsoft Azure Privacy Policy.

Sales of Personal Information

Brigadier has never sold personal information. In addition, Brigadier has no intention of ever selling personal information.

Data Retention Policy

Data will only be retained until you request us to delete it pursuant to this privacy policy.

Securing Your Data

Brigadier will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

Cross-border Data Transfers

Information that Brigadier collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which Brigadier operates to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy. You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.

Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 18 or knowingly allow such persons to register with the newsletter. If you are under 18, please do not send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 18 is allowed to provide any personal information to or on Brigadier. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 18 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 18, please contact us.

GDPR Compliance

Brigadier seeks to fully comply with the European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other laws and regulations protecting consumer privacy. As such, you have: the right to be forgotten (you may cancel your subscription to the newsletter at any time. If you wish your data to be permanently deleted, you may contact us at support@brigadier.club and it will be deleted within 72 hours); the right to object (we have never performed any data science projects on user data and do not anticipate doing so in the future. If, at some point in the future we determine a use for any sort of data science project on our user data, we will notify you in writing before doing so. In that event you may remove your contact data from any such project by emailing us at support@brigadier.club with the subject line “Opt Out of Data Science”); right to access and rectification and right of portability (you can contact us at support@brigadier.club at any time to access copies of all user data we retain for you. We will remove, update, or change any personal information about you from our servers immediately upon your request); right of restriction (you can make a request for restriction or suppression of your personal data through the contact information at the end of this privacy policy);

Without prejudice to any other administrative or judicial remedy, if located in the European Union, you shall have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the Member State of your habitual residence, place of work or place of the alleged infringement if you consider that the processing of personal data relating to you infringes the GDPR.

In addition to the above, an “unsubscribe” option is automatically included in the footer of every newsletter sent from Brigadier. This allows you to easily unsubscribe from the newsletter. In the event you unsubscribe, we will immediately stop sending you the newsletter. Brigadier will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your GDPR or other consumer privacy rights. Unless permitted by law, Brigadier will not deny you goods or services nor provide you a different level or quality of goods or services. However, the nature of the services provided by Brigadier require basic information including your portfolio and email address. Should you opt out entirely, Brigadier will be unable to provide you any of its services.

You have the right to request that Brigadier disclose certain information to you about our collection and use of your personal information over the past 12 months. Once we receive and confirm your verifiable consumer request, we will disclose to you: The categories of personal information Brigadier collected about you; The categories of sources for the personal information Brigadier collected about you; Brigadier’s business or commercial purpose for collecting or selling that personal information; The categories of third parties with whom Brigadier shares that personal information; The specific pieces of personal information Brigadier collected about you (also called a data portability request); Deletion Request Rights; If Brigadier sold or disclosed your personal information for a business purpose two separate lists disclosing: sales, identifying the personal information categories that each category of recipient purchased; and disclosures for a business purpose, identifying the personal information categories that each category of recipient obtained.

You have the right to request that Brigadier delete any of your personal information that Brigadier collected from you and retained, subject to certain exceptions. Once Brigadier receives and confirms your verifiable consumer request, Brigadier will delete (and direct our service providers to delete) your personal information, unless an exception applies. Brigadier may deny your deletion request if retaining the information is necessary for Brigadier to: Complete the transaction for which we collected the personal information, provide a good or service that you requested, take actions reasonably anticipated within the context of our ongoing business relationship with you, or otherwise perform our contract with you. Detect security incidents, protect against malicious, deceptive, fraudulent, or illegal activity, or prosecute those responsible for such activities. Debug products to identify and repair errors that impair existing intended functionality. Comply with a legal obligation. Make other internal and lawful uses of that information that are compatible with the context in which you provided it. Exercising Access, Data Portability, and Deletion Rights

To exercise the access, data portability, and deletion rights described above, please submit a verifiable consumer request to us by contacting us at support@brigadier.club. Please provide sufficient information that allows Brigadier to reasonably verify you are the person about whom Brigadier collected personal information or an authorized representative. Describe your request with sufficient detail that allows Brigadier to properly understand, evaluate, and respond to it. Brigadier cannot respond to your request or provide you with personal information if Brigadier cannot verify your identity or authority to make the request and confirm the personal information relates to you. Brigadier will only use personal information provided in a verifiable consumer request to verify the requestor’s identity or authority to make the request.

Response Timing and Format

Brigadier endeavors to respond to a verifiable consumer request within thirty (30) days of its receipt. If we require more time (up to 90 days), Brigadier will inform you of the reason and extension period in writing. Any disclosures Brigadier provides will only cover the 12-month period preceding the verifiable consumer request’s receipt. The response Brigadier provides will also explain the reasons we cannot comply with a request, if applicable. For data portability requests, Brigadier will select a format to provide your personal information that is readily useable and should allow you to transmit the information from one entity to another entity without hindrance. Brigadier does not charge a fee to process or respond to your verifiable consumer request unless it is excessive, repetitive, or manifestly unfounded. If Brigadier determine that the request warrants a fee, Stratechery will tell you why it made that decision and provide you with a cost estimate before completing your request.

Updating This Statement

Brigadier may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

Contact Brigadier

If you have any questions, complaints, or requests concerning your data, this privacy policy, or Brigadier’s treatment of your personal information, please write to support@brigadier.club
