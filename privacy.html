Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Brigadier collects, and the ways in which it uses that personal information.

Personal Information Collection

At Brigaider.club, if you sign up to receive the newsletter, you provide your name, surname and email address, plus ticker and percentage of the portfolio you would like to monitor. That is the only information we have for you and all we need to operate brigadier.club. We do not collect any information on other visitors to the website. We use third third-party vendors, identified below, to help with delivery of the newsletter.

Legal Basis for Collection of Information

Brigadier asserts the following legal grounds for the collection of data: 1) Explicit Consent - When you sign up for the newsletter, you provide explicit consent for Brigadier to collect the above listed personal information; 2) Legitimate Interests - Brigadier may have additional legitimate interests, including account maintenance and administrative purposes, for the collection of the above listed personal information.

Using Personal Information

Brigadier may use your personal information to fulfill or meet the reason you provided the information. Specifically, delivering the newsletter and account-related email. We may also use your personal information to: provide you with support and to respond to your inquiries, including to investigate and address your concerns and monitor and improve our responses; administer website and content. respond to law enforcement requests and as required by applicable law, court order, or governmental regulations; and send you marketing communications. Brigadier does not share any of its user data with any third parties except the third-party vendors identified below. In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified in this privacy policy, Brigadier may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Data Processors

Brigadier uses the following data processors with respect to the personal information collected: Microsoft Azure to host the website, deliver the newsletter, and all related user login data and analytics. Microsoft Azure Privacy Policy.

Sales of Personal Information

Brigadier has never sold personal information. In addition, Brigadier has no intention of ever selling personal information.

Data Retention Policy

Data will only be retained until you request us to delete it pursuant to this privacy policy.

Securing Your Data

Brigadier will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

Cross-border Data Transfers

Information that Brigadier collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which Brigadier operates to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy. You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.

Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 18 or knowingly allow such persons to register with the newsletter. If you are under 18, please do not send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 18 is allowed to provide any personal information to or on Stratechery. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 18 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 18, please contact us.
